Popcat Privacy Policy

Data Collection:

Our app, Popcat Saga, does not collect any personal data or any other data from its users. We do not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not track your activities within the app or outside of it.

No Data Storage or Sharing by Us

Since our app does not collect any user data, we do not store, process, or share any of your data with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe that we may have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
