3072 Privacy Policy

Data Collection:

Our app, 3072, does not collect any personal data or any other data from its users. We do not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not track your activities within the app or outside of it.

Third-Party Services

While our app itself does not collect data, it does utilize Google AdMob, an advertising service provided by Google. AdMob may collect and use certain information to provide advertisements.

Google AdMob: AdMob may collect information such as:
  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system, unique device identifiers).
  • IP address.
  • Information about ads served, viewed, or clicked.
  • Other information as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

AdMob uses this information to deliver relevant advertisements. Please note that we, as the app developer, do not have access to or control over the data collected by Google AdMob.

For more information on how Google AdMob collects and uses data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy

No Data Storage or Sharing by Us

Since our app does not collect any user data, we do not store, process, or share any of your data with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe that we may have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
